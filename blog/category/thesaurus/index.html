Atramhasis v0.5.0 released

  • Sep 14, 2016
  • Koen van Daele
  • English
  • releases, thesaurus, open source
  • atramhasis

A new version (0.5.0) of Atramhasis has been released. Atramhasis is an open-source, web-based SKOS editor. You can use it to browse SKOS vocabularies, thesauri, authority files, word lists, ... Atramhasis also comes with an admin interface you can use to add the concepts and collections of your vocabularies.
